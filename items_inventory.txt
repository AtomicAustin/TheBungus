Items/inventory

Item class- base class for all items. Items are drawn/positioned on the map until collided with
then they're moved into the inventory
	. position
	. drop
	. sprite
	. tag

Item Manager- class used for managing items on the map 
	. Update position
	. draw to screen
	. collision
	. get tag
	. set tag
	. add to inventory
	. add to manager
	. remove from manager

Inventory GUI- sets up and displays the inventory GUI
	. Set up rectangles
	. track position

Inventory Manager- manages items in the inventory
	. add item to inventory
	. remove item from inventory
	. get item in inventory
	. display items in inventory 
	. set item position
	. track mouse hovers
